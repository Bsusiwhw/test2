Southern Metrology and Calibration Services, Inc. is an ISO/IEC 17025:2017 accreditted Calibration laboratory.

As experts in metrology and calibration, we give you, your associates, and customers the confidence that your measurement procedures are always performed in accordance with your quality requirements. Our services also works as a quality label, that your test equipment is calibrated by an independent, qualified laboratory. You can be assured of top-notch performance delivery and professional quality services coming from our customer-focused and knowledgeable staff (with over twenty (20) years of metrology and calibration application experience), and we’ll be responsive to your needs.

Our state of the art facilities and wide variety of Metrology and Calibration equipment can cater a small, to medium, to large enterprise. Feel free to contact us any time if you have questions or inquiries. The company is accredited by a number of accreditation bureaus and other government agencies.

Our metrology and calibration services are performed on-site at your facilities or in our state-of-the-art laboratories. And as part of our objectives, we implement turnaround time of five (5) to fifteen (15) working days, have the RECALL system, and provide logistics for collection, pick-up and delivery services.
